Care Services

Cardinal Home Health offers a comprehensive range of in-home care services designed to meet your unique needs.

Consumer Directed Services

If you are suffering from injury or illness, Cardinal provides the opportunity to hire your own personal care assistant, such as a family member or friend. This person will be paid to look after you in the comfort of your own home.

Personal In-Home Care

Cardinal's caregivers provide the highest quality service to bring comfort to you or your loved one. Any problems will be effectively and pleasantly dealt with to assure safe and smooth interactions. This service includes:

  • Meal preparation
  • Household Chores
  • Dressing and Grooming
  • Medication assistance and supervision
  • Respite Care
  • Bathing
  • Ostomy care
  • Catheter care

Private Duty

This program offers the opportunity to pay for services which are not covered under the state funding. If you are concerned about your own health or a loved one's health and want an expert to oversee their daily routines, then this program provides the perfect solution.
